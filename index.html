04 Brand Guidelines

What the brand stands for.

These rules protect the integrity of the Jardin Délices identity across every touchpoint — digital, print, physical, and social.

Brand Voice & Personality
Elegant
Not: Pretentious
Refined language, never cold. The brand speaks with quiet confidence — it doesn't shout or over-explain. Every word earns its place.
Warm
Not: Casual
Inviting and human, but always polished. The tone of a host who makes you feel immediately at ease, without ever losing composure.
Botanical
Not: Rustic
Nature-inspired but curated. The brand references gardens, blooms, and growth — always in elevated, artistic language, never earthy or rough.
Usage Rules
✓ Do
Always use Cormorant Garamond for the logo wordmark and primary display text. The italic weight carries the brand's French character.
✗ Don't
Never substitute the display font with a sans-serif. This strips the elegance and French heritage from the brand identity.
✓ Do
Use Pantone 5787C sage green as the dominant brand colour in all applications — it's the colour of the space itself.
✗ Don't
Never use the bloom orange as a primary background colour. It is an accent — supporting the sage, not competing with it.
✓ Do
Always use warm white (#F8F6F2) as the default page background — not pure white. It mirrors the warm marble walls of the restaurant.
✗ Don't
Never use pure #FFFFFF white in brand materials. It reads as clinical and disconnects from the warm, organic space.
✓ Do
Use the accent on Délices (é) consistently in all brand materials. It is part of the proper French name and signals authenticity.
✗ Don't
Never write "Jardin Delices" without the accent in official brand communications, digital or print.
✓ Do
Maintain generous whitespace around the logo. The minimum clear space is equal to the height of the capital "J" on all sides.
✗ Don't
Never place the logo on a busy photographic background without an adequate overlay or isolation zone.
